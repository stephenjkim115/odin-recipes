Scrambled Eggs

Description

Scrambled eggs are cheap, easy-to-make, nutritious, and delicious. I learned the recipe below from my good friends in college.

Ingredients

  • 6 large eggs
  • sea salt, to taste
  • ground black pepper, to taste
  • 2 tbsp extra-virgin olive oil
  • sesame oil, to taste

Steps

  1. Heat pan on stovetop to medium heat. Add olive oil and spread around pan.
  2. Turn down heat to low-medium and add eggs. As eggs are cooking, use a spatula to consistently and gently prevent the eggs from fully cooking.
  3. Eventually, despite your mixing, the eggs will begin to solidify. Add salt,pepper, and sesame oil (to taste) at this point.
  4. Before the eggs look completely cooked, remove the pan from the stovetop. The residual heat will cook the remainder of the eggs.
  5. Enjoy!
